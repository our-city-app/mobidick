Mobidick: Rogerthat sample service

To use the mobidick sample Rogerthat service implementation, login in your Rogerthat control panel @ https://rogerth.at.

If your service would not yet be configured for mobidick, click the "Use mobidick sample service" in the upper right corner of your Rogerthat service panel. (You might need to clear the callback configuration to make the link apear.)

After configuring mobidick as your service implementation, create your mobidick session via the "Open mobidick sample service control panel" link in the upper right corner of your Rogerthat control panel.
